The Wedding Day

06.11.21

Marriage

09.30am onwards

The processional begins with the grandparents, flows through the parents, groom, officiant, wedding party, flower girl, and ring bearer, and ends with the bride making her entrance.

Words of Welcome

12.30pm onwards

Once everyone is in place, the officiant will say a few words of welcome. He or she may thank guests for bearing witness to your union, as well as welcome everyone to our venue for celebration.

Photography

2.00pm onwards

the officiant will offer an introduction and some thoughts on marriage. This could be a brief recounting of your love story, words on what marriage means to you, or a statement about the ceremony to come and represents.

Wedding Cake

3.30pm onwards

A wedding cake is the traditional cake served at wedding receptions following dinner.The wedding cake is served at a wedding breakfast; the 'wedding breakfast' does not mean the meal will be held in the morning before the wedding.

Wedding Party

4.00pm onwards

At the wedding party the officiant introduces the married couple for the first time. Then, the bride and groom lead the recessional back up the aisle as guests shower you with the rice!
